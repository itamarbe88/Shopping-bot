Privacy Policy for Salvador

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Overview

Salvador is a household grocery management app. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

  • Account information: your name and email address via Google Sign-In
  • Household data: grocery items, shopping lists, and inventory you enter in the app
  • Camera: used only to photograph grocery items you choose to add to your inventory

How We Use Your Data

  • To sync your grocery lists and inventory across devices
  • To identify your household and share data with household members you invite
  • We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Google Cloud Storage in the EU (europe-west1 region).

Data Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data, visit our Delete Account page. Your account and all data will be permanently deleted within 7 days.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In (authentication)
  • Google Cloud Storage (data storage)
  • Google Firebase (authentication services)
